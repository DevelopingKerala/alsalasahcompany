OCEAN FREIGHT IMPORT AND EXPORT DOOR TO DOOR SERVICE

We have worldwide freight agency network to cater ocean forwarding services to clients in Kuwait and all major overseas destinations. Our operational expertise and experience in ocean forwarding offers best forwarding solutions to our clients.

AIR FREIGHT IMPORT AND EXPORT

Our excellent relations with Airlines & agents coupled with efficient and precise mediation with clients bring remarkable quality in air freight forwarding to provide reliable and safe air freight services. Our forwarding expertise helps trimming down unnecessary chargeable volumes in air shipments.

OVERLAND TRANSPORTATION IMPORT AND EXPORT BETWEEN EUROPE AND MIDDLE EAST

More than 30 years experience in overland traffic within the Middle East region, we know the issues of cross boarder traffic like documentary requirements, Customs and security inspections, Customs brokers services etc. A fast boarder crossing ends up in a earliar arrival at destination.

PROJECT FORWARDING & LOGISTICS

We have a crew of seasoned personnel in project handling and forwarding and their enormous experience in handling large volume projects cargoes and special equipment made us a specialist in handling project cargo over the years. The group and its crew had involved in a variety of porjects in Kuwait and the region for local clients, government projects and international clients. We were engaged in trucking distribution of products of Petrochemical Industries, pipe transportation for oil sector, logistic support for U.S. Governmental contractors like KBR, Japanese Aid for Iraq for rebuilding power stations and substations in Iraq, power stations in Kuwait and Iraq, rebuilding of Iraqi Port of Umm Qasr, haulage of airport boarding bridges, relocation of factory machinery, etc. We are capable of Logistics Project management and handling large volume project cargo in a very professional way.

CUSTOMS CLEARANCE & LOCAL TRANSPORTATION

We have our own vehicles for meeting our transportation needs in Kuwait. We are engaged in surface transportation since the last 3 decades under the business group. We are a licensed Customs broker in Kuwait to assist our customers with the best possible Customs exit in terms of taxes and time for bill of entry

WAREHOUSING & SAFEKEEPING

We have own warehouse with 2000 SQM basement storage with easy access 24/7 basis. We are providing these services with reasonable fees to our clients.

THIRD PARTY INSPECTION & INSURANCE

In conjunction with internationally reputed agents and qualified people we provide inspection, certifications and services to our client.
